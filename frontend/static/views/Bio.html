Bio

I'm Leevi Vahvelainen and I study design in Aalto university. My focus is in industrial, service and strategic design for the design process itself.

As a designer, I seek to, understand user experiences and actions to create fluent usability in easily understandable form. For me, design starts from the base purpose witch I keep in mind to come up with holistic solutions. I always consider the implementation of manufacturing methods to strategy and practical arrangements. I believe flexible plans will carry out most successfully. Aesthetically, I believe elegant and identifiable forms are the most sustainable ones, unless when the purpose is to stand-out.
